In The Winter Meadow

by: Emiliya Volchenko

Emiliya Volchenko (she/her) is a Graphic Design student at OCAD University with a minor in Photography. Her artistic exploration delves into themes of identity and healing in nature through a multimedia approach.

         

OCAD University acknowledges the ancestral territories of the Mississaugas of the Credit, the Haudenosaunee, the Anishinaabeg and the Huron-Wendat, who are the original owners and custodians of the land on which we live, work and create.
